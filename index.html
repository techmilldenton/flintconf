What is FlintConf?

This is a one-day, single track conference in Denton for entrepreneurs, creatives, freelancers, side hustlers, and students in any stage of their business development to learn how to optimize available resources to start and grow successfully. Conference attendees will participate in business-informative, interactive sessions lead by experts in entrepreneurship, lean startup, marketing, branding, finance, wellness, and more.

Wherever you are in your entrepreneurial pursuits, FlintConf will take you further.

Who is FlintConf for?

Hipsters

design, creative, art, music, makers

For art and creativity to become sustenance: Feed the fire and yourself.

Hackers

development, engineering, technology

For code to become cash: Develop ways to translate your work and value.

Hustlers

sales, marketing, bizdev

For ideas to become tangible: Transform ideas into plans and plans into results.
