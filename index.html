What is flintconf?

Learn how to get started and burn hot at FlintConf, a one-day conference of insightful speakers, engaging panels, and interactive breakout sessions in Denton, TX. Hear from experts in branding, marketing, fundraising, and more to strengthen the skills you need to spark your ideas into being or fan the fire.

This is a one-day, single track conference in Denton for entrepreneurs, creatives, freelancers, side hustlers, and students in any stage of their business development to learn how to optimize available resources to start and grow a successful business. Conference attendees will participate in business-informative, interactive sessions lead by experts in entrepreneurship, lean startup, marketing, branding, finance, product development, human resources, and more.

The 2019 FlintConf will be the first conference in Denton to bring together entrepreneurs, creatives, and freelancers. The intention is to educate and connect entrepreneurs to the resources, ideas, and people that they need to take their business to the next level, whether that business is art, music, technology, service, or a consumer product. Through a day of speakers, panels, break out panels, and networking opportunities we hope to educate, empower, and inspire you to strengthen and grow your business.
